Minecraft Modpacks

I used to make a bunch of modpacks for my server back in the day. While most of them just slap together vaguely related mods,
there are a few old ones and some newer ones i liked enough to put them on here.

World of Life - Rekindled (2025) is an updated version of my old World of Life pack.
Its supposed to be scummy and hard, so i don't mind it being a basic pack.

Kingdoms and Castles - Rekindled (2025) is a medieval, rpg inspired modpack with detailed quest progression and loads of bosses and unique loot.
It even has some cozy elements for medieval roleplay.
Technically it's not an updated version of an old modpack, but a melting pot of all my old, unfocused, medieval type modpacks

The Invasion Pack (2020) still isnt completed, but ill get back to it in time. It's a small modpack around the invasion mod,
tasking players to defend thier nexus from smarter mobs for as many waves as possible.

If youre still interested in the old modpacks, i archived most of them on this old website. Its in german tho.

Minecraft Mods

Pixelbreaker Expansion 2 (Mirror) (2022-23) Was the last MCreator mod i worked on before i took a break from modded minecraft.
Its full of misc. features that enhance modded play.

Pixelbreaker Expansion (Mirror) (2019-21) was the first big mod i worked on. So naturally, it became really messy and i had to start from scratch.

I also created 2 smaller mods that got integrated into the Expansion later on. Voxel's Coin Economy (2021), which later turned into emerald shards,
and Harder Cobblestone (2019), which was a feature in the old expansion, before deepslate was a thing.
